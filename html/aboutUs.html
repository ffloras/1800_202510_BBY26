About Us

At Paws & Homes, we are on a heartfelt mission to give every pet a loving home and to create a world where animals are treated with kindness and respect. We are a non-profit dedicated to rescuing, caring for, and finding forever families for pets in need across the region.

Since our founding in 2018, we’ve worked tirelessly to make a difference in the lives of animals. From stray dogs and cats to small animals like rabbits and guinea pigs, we believe every pet deserves a chance to be loved. Our team is passionate about building stronger bonds between pets and people through adoption, education, and community support.

Our Story

Paws & Homes began as a small initiative by a group of friends who couldn’t bear to see animals suffering on the streets. What started as a tiny rescue operation in a local garage has grown into a network of care facilities dedicated to animal welfare. Today, we proudly operate:

  • 4 pet adoption shelters in California, Texas, and Arizona, where we care for animals until they find their perfect match.
  • 1 mobile veterinary unit that travels to rural areas, offering free health checkups and vaccinations.
  • 2 foster care programs in Los Angeles and Houston, supporting temporary homes for pets in transition.
  • A pet behavior training center in San Diego, helping pets adjust to their new families.
  • Our headquarters in Austin, where we manage adoption events, volunteer programs, and outreach initiatives.

What We Do

We offer a variety of programs to support pets and their families, ensuring every animal gets the care they need:

  • Rescue and Adoption: We rescue pets from high-risk situations and find them loving homes through our adoption process.
  • Pet Health Services: Affordable vaccinations, microchipping, and emergency care for pets in our shelters and the community.
  • Behavioral Support: Training sessions to help pets overcome anxiety and adapt to their new environments.
  • Community Outreach: Free workshops on pet care, adoption fairs, and school programs to teach kids about compassion for animals.
  • Foster Network: A growing network of foster families who provide temporary homes for pets awaiting adoption.

Our Impact

Over the years, Paws & Homes has transformed countless lives. Here’s a glimpse of what we’ve achieved:

  • Helped 25,000+ pets find their forever homes through adoption.
  • Provided medical care to over 15,000 animals, including vaccinations and surgeries.
  • Trained 5,000 pets to improve their behavior and increase their chances of adoption.
  • Educated 50,000+ community members on responsible pet ownership and animal welfare.

Our work is made possible by the generosity of donors, volunteers, and partners who share our vision of a world where every pet has a home. Learn more about our journey in our impact report.

Our Values

At Paws & Homes, we are guided by a set of core values that shape everything we do:

  • Compassion: We treat every animal with the love and care they deserve.
  • Community: We believe in the power of people coming together to make a difference.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency in all our actions.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new ways to improve the lives of pets and their families.
